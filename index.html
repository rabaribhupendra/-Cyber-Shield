💡 2. Essential Cyber Security Tips

  • 🔐 Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Always enable Two-Factor Verification (2FA) on your Email, Instagram, and Banking accounts. Even if your password is stolen, your account remains secure.
  • 🌐 Verify HTTP vs HTTPS Whenever you open a website, check if it starts with 'https://' and displays a Padlock symbol. Never enter personal details on websites that use only 'http://'.
  • ⚠️ Beware of Urgency Scams Messages creating fear and panic—such as "Your SIM card will be blocked" or "Your electricity connection will be disconnected tonight"—demanding immediate link clicks are always fraudulent.
  • 🛑 Fake Lotteries and Free Gift Links Phishing links claiming "You won a $10,000 lottery" or offering "Free high-end smartphones" are traps designed to hack your phone or drain your bank account.
